About Ailani Orchards...

Ailani Orchards (meaning "to eat heaven" or "spiritual food") is an organic farm on the Southern-most portion of the Big Island. The mild climate offers the perfect amount sun and rain while the deep, rich, volcanic soil in the Ka'u district provides an ideal growing condition for our orchard.

Our Organic Products...

Everything grown at Ailani Orchards is 100% chemical and pesticide free, far surpassing USDA's Organic Standards.
We grow coffee, macadamia nuts, avocados, a wide varierty of citrus fruits, as well as, apples, bananas, pineapples, star fruit, and guava.

Purchase Our Products...

Ailani Orchards is delighted to offer our organic macadamia nut and coffee products available for online purchase; Unfortunately, dues to the restrictions of the USDA, in order to enjoy our organically grown fruits, you will need to visit us on the Big Island of Hawai'i, where we're ready to welcome you with open arms.
